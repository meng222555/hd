Eligibility to Rent

  • Rent a HDB Unit

  • Rent a Private Unit

Note: HDB terms "renting" the whole flat as "subletting" the whole flat

 

Check Citizenship of Landlord

Only Singapore Citizens are allowed to sublet the entire HDB flat to you.

Singapore Permanent Residents (SPRs) are not allowed to sublet their flat to you.

 

Check Landlord's Minimum Occupation Period (MOP) of 5 years

Landlord has to fulfill the 5 years MOP before he can sublet the whole flat to you.

Ask landlord for a printed confirmation letter from HDB of his eligibility to sublet the whole flat.

 

Non-Citizen Quota for Subletting of Flat

This quota is not applicable to you if you are Singaporean and Malaysian.

If you are a non-Malaysian non-citizen (Singapore Permanent Resident or foreigner), you have to check that this quota is not reached for you to rent a flat at the specified flat.

You may use HDB e-Service to check if you are eligible to rent a flat at the specified flat.

 

Citizenship

You as the subtenant must be any of the following:

  • Singapore Citizens
  • Singapore Permanent Residents
  • Non-citizens legally residing in Singapore who are holders of Employment Passes, S Passes, Work Permits, Student Passes, Dependant Passes, or Long-Term Social Visit Passes. These passes must have a validity period of at least 6 months as at the date of your subletting application
    • Work Permit holders from the construction, marine and process sectors must be Malaysians
    • Tourists are not allowed as subtenants of HDB flats

Tenancy and property ownership status

You as subtenant must not be tenant or owner of other HDB flat unless you are:

  • Divorced/ legally separated. In this case, only 1 party can be a subtenant
  • Owners who are eligible to sublet their whole flat. However, they must sublet their own flat within a month after having sublet a flat from another flat owner

You as subtenant must not be owner of Executive Condominium unit where the 5-year MOP has not been met.

 

 

Please call HDB Branch Office 1800 225 5432 if you need any clarifications

Consideration to Rent a Private Property

Please take note that the minimum rental period of residential property in Singapore is 6 months.

Please ensure the validity of your pass and/or document to stay in Singapore legally. Your landlord can check the validity from the various governing bodies.

  • NRIC, Student Pass or Long Term Social Visit Pass
  • Work permit
  • S Pass or Employment Pass
